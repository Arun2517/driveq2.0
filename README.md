# 🚗 DriveQ 2.0

> **Next Generation Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) Communication System**
>
> **Currently Under Research & Development**

---

## 📖 Overview

DriveQ 2.0 is the next-generation evolution of the original **DriveQ** project, extending the capabilities of Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) communication through advanced wireless communication technologies, intelligent transportation concepts, and AI-assisted decision-making.

The project aims to develop a secure, scalable, and low-latency communication platform that enhances road safety, improves emergency message dissemination, and supports future intelligent transportation systems (ITS).

## 🎯 Objectives

- Develop a reliable V2V communication framework.
- Reduce communication latency.
- Improve vehicle safety through cooperative awareness.
- Support emergency message broadcasting.
- Enhance communication security.
- Integrate Artificial Intelligence for intelligent decision making.
- Explore future Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X) technologies.
